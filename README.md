# EXCEL-DASHBOARD
This project presents an interactive Supermart Grocery Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel. It analyzes sales, profit, and discount trends across different product categories and regions. The dashboard uses Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and slicers to provide clear business insights and track yearly sales performance.

# 📊 Supermart Grocery Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive sales dashboard built using Microsoft Excel** to analyze grocery store sales data.  
The dashboard helps identify **sales trends, regional performance, product profitability, and discount patterns**.

Using **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers**, users can easily explore the data and gain meaningful business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
This dashboard answers the following business questions:

1. Which category has the most sales?  
2. How much have sales increased year by year?  
3. How do sales compare across different regions?  
4. Which category gives the highest discount?  
5. Which category generates the highest profit?  
6. How is sales distributed across regions?  

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard highlights important business metrics:

- **Total Sales (Overall Revenue):** 14,956,982  
- **Total Number of Orders:** 9,994  

These KPIs provide a quick summary of business performance.

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1️⃣ Sales by Category
Shows total sales generated by each product category such as:
- Bakery  
- Beverages  
- Eggs, Meat & Fish  
- Food Grains  
- Fruits & Vegetables  
- Oil & Masala  
- Snacks  

Helps identify the **top-performing categories**.

---

### 2️⃣ Sales by City & Region
Displays sales across different **cities and regions**, helping understand geographical sales performance.

---

### 3️⃣ Average Discount by Category
Shows the **average discount percentage** offered for each product category.

---

### 4️⃣ Yearly Sales Trend
Tracks **sales growth from 2015 to 2018**, helping analyze business growth over time.

---

### 5️⃣ Sales Distribution by Region
A pie chart showing the **percentage contribution of each region** to total sales.

---

### 6️⃣ Profit by Category
Shows the **profit generated by each product category**, helping identify high-profit segments.

---
